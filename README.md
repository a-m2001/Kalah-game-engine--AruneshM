My current engine is a decision-making machine.

Minimax defines WHAT optimal play means.

Alpha-Beta makes it feasible.

Iterative Deepening controls search.

Zobrist + TT prevent repeated work... (memoization step kinda.. zobrist makes it feasible to cache, and actual memo happens in Transposition Tables)

Move Ordering + Killer Moves improve pruning... (Heuristics algo to increase efficiency of alpha beta pruning)

PV explains the decision... (and decides on the best move .. opinionated decision)

Endgame Solver removes guessing near the finish.

Together they approximate mathematically-perfect play possible.
